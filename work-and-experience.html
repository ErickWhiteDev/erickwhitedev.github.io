Omitron Internship - NASA CARA

I am currently an analysis intern for Omitron, a NASA contractor, on the CARA program. I mainly work on updating and developing unit tests for various routines in the CARA SDK, as well as improving the safety and robustness of some of these routines. As part of this internship, I have gained extensive exposure to various facets of astrodynamics, both in practical and theoretical aspects of the field itself (i.e., element set/reference frame/time system conversions, two line element sets, orbit shapes/parameters/maneuvers, precession/nutation, etc.) and more generally applicable topics (i.e., covariance/error, MATLAB unit testing, engineering practices, etc.).
